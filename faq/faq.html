Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I book a single room?

    The majority of trips offer individual rooms; to find out if one is offered, see the Essential Trip Information or the trip overview page on our website. If so, kindly request it when making the reservation.

    Can we book a triple room?

    Depending on the type of vacation you select, you might be able to request a triple room. While you can ask for a triple room when making your reservation, it can't be guaranteed because of how far in advance we make our reservations.

    I'm travelling with a partner, can I make sure we have a double bed?

    If you’re travelling with a partner, just let us know you want to share a room (and a bed) at the time of booking. We can’t guarantee a double bed, but we’ll do our best to accommodate you. In some destinations, the option may be two single beds pushed together to form a ‘double’.

  • I want to cancel my booking, how can I do this?

    To cancel, kindly contact us via live chat. Only after receiving written authorization from you may we proceed with a cancellation. Depending on the departure date, you can also be charged a cancellation fee.

    I want to cancel my booking, can I get a full refund?

    If you decide to cancel, we won't give you a complete refund. However, you have 56 days before departure to cancel your reservation and either transfer your money to another trip or keep it as a credit for future travel.

    How long will it take for my refund to be credited to my credit card?

    Although we make every effort to process refunds swiftly, it can take 14 to 21 days depending on your bank and outside processes that are beyond our control. Please get in touch with your bank if the money doesn't appear in your account within 21 days.

  • Are Transportation Fee included in the Tour Price?

    Our package does not include any tranportation, however, if you wish to include transportation in your travels we may help you to secure them. If this were the case we would incur transportation fee accordingly.

  • Why do you take full Payment?

    Because we need to clarify every detail of your excursion with our local operators and suppliers as we go, we ask for full payment for some itineraries (e.g. on-tour transport, activities and accommodation). If we can't accommodate you on the trip, we'll give you a full refund.

    When is the final due date for my trip?

    Full payment is required 56 days before departure to avoid trip cancellation.

    When i fail to made payment

    Oh no! We would love to assist you in planning your upcoming journey. Insufficient funds, inaccurate payment information, or problems with the internet connection can all prevent online payments through our booking engine from being processed. After reviewing the information, if your payment is still not being processed, please contact us by phone or live chat.

  • Do you offer vegetarian choices?

    Yes,but you have to inform us two weeks prior to your trip so that we can arrange accordingly with the hotel.

  • Will i have to wear a mask when travelling?

    All travellers must wear masks the entire time they are travelling, as directed by local health officials. We recommend that all travellers to wear masks at all times as to help reduce the spread of COVID-19.

    What happens if someone on a trip show symptoms of covid-19

    Our will help customers find medical care if they become ill while travelling. Customers are also encouraged to bring their own RAT tests from home as a backup, and we may provide COVID-19 testing if they want it.
