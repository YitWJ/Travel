About Us

Our company name is Discovery.Our company works as a travel agency. Discovery Travel Agency is taking travelers to visit the famous tourist destinations in Malaysia. Branches of our company are located in Kuala Lumpur and Pahang.

The one day travel plan's destinations that our company provides are Pulau Pinang, Ipoh, Kuala Lumpur, Melaka in Peninsular Malaysia and Sabah, Sarawak.

Discovery Travel Agency is a one-stop travel agency. Travelers don't need to worry about anything, our company will pick you up at our branch and lead travelers to the destination. Our company will solve every problem, travelers just need to enjoy the journey and act in concert with our travel arrangement. We promise that your trip will go well and undisturbed. Our travel agency also provides travel insurance for travelers to ensure your safety and this insurance is already included in the travel plan for free, travelers don't have to buy other travel insurance.

The one day trip plan will bring travelers to visit the famous tourist attractions in the state. Travelers can enjoy the local scrumptious foods and also experience the local culture. Our company has a translator following the trip. Travelers that have communication problems when traveling can contact us. Our translator will help you patiently and try to solve your problem.
