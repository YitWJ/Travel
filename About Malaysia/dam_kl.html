KUALA LUMPUR


Kuala Lumpur is the capital city of Malaysia. Kuala Lumpur is named
in Malay means “muddy estuary”, it's also known as KL.
Kuala Lumpur also is a commercial, business, entertainment, shopping, educational,
urban living, tourist destination, and was once the country's administrative center.

Kuala Lumpur is located by the Klang and Gombak Rivers.
Petronas Twin Towers is the icon of Kuala Lumpur.
It is 52 meters and 88 stories high, and is the tallest twin building in the world.
